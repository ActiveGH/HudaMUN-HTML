Agenda Item: State of Missouri v. Gypsy Rose Blanchard & Nicholas Godejohn

Step into the courtroom where justice hangs in the balance and every word matters. The Missouri State Criminal Court is not just a place of legal procedure—it is a battleground of truth, motive, and consequence. This special committee will examine one of the most harrowing and complex cases in recent American criminal history: the trial of Gypsy Rose Blanchard and Nicholas Godejohn.

As attorneys, witnesses, and judges, delegates will explore the murky intersection of abuse, manipulation, and murder. Was it a cry for freedom, or a premeditated crime? You will reconstruct testimonies, dissect evidence, and debate morality under the sharp light of the law. Every choice in this courtroom shapes the verdict, and the stakes are nothing less than justice itself.

Will you uphold the rule of law—or redefine it? Court is now in session.Study Guide
